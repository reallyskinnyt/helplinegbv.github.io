ABOUT US



Helpline GBV ensures that victims are empowered and their voices are heard. Gender Based Violence in South Africa mostly affects women and this platform strives to combat GBV or any kind of abuse against women and children. Eveyone is welcomed and encouraged to report any kind of abuse using this platform without any fear. A great number of victims do not report cases out of fear for the perpetrators and that needs to change.


Helpline GBV is the bridge between victims and the necessary help they need. At Helpline GBV we alert the authories of GBV taking place and protect the identity of the person reporting should they want to remain anonymous. GBV is not only limited to women but it includes all genders in the country. There are different forms of Gender Based Violence and this platform caters for all forms: Violence against Women and Girls (VAWG), Violence against LGBTI people, Domestic Violence (DV), Sexual Violence (SV), and the most common one is Intimate Partner Violence (IPV). The platform also offers necessary counseling information for victims and children affected by abuse. To ensure a gender based violence free future society, the mental health of children needs to be protected as they are the future of this country.
